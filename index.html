Md. Ariful Islam Sanim

Ph.D. Student in Statistics · Graduate Teaching Assistant

University of South Carolina, Columbia

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Statistics at the University of South Carolina, advised by Dr. William Consagra. My research develops functional data analysis and localized methods for complex, high-dimensional data — including localized principal component analysis over two-dimensional manifolds, causal inference, Bayesian methods, and generative (diffusion) models — with applications to neuroimaging and wearable-device data.
