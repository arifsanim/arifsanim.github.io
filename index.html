Hi, I am Ariful Islam Sanim. I am a Ph.D. Student in the Department of Statistics at the University of South Carolina. I am advised by Dr. William Consagra, and I work as a Graduate Teaching Assistant in the Department of Statistics. My Ph.D. research focuses on using functional data analysis in localized regression models, statistical learning, and the development of efficient methodologies for analyzing complex, high-dimensional data. My interests include functional regression, nonparametric methods, machine learning, and their applications to real-world problems.
